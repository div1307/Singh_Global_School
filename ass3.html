About

History and Vision of Singh's Global School

Welcome! Singh's Global School has been featuring among the top 10 schools of East Delhi and top 20 schools of Delhi for the last 5 years (2019-2024). It provides education to the students from classes Nursery to 12th (all streams - Science, Commerce, Humanities). It was established in the year 1995 by the present Chairman Mr. Kulwinder Singh in Mayur Vihar, Delhi-91. His vision remains the same ,which is reflected in the school's logo 'Little hands with their green acts to make this earth a better place to live'. He wanted to provide quality education to students to build a better future both for themselves and the Earth and continues to succeed in his imagined vision with over 3000 students and a faculty of over 100 selectively chosen teachers. He began the school in tents and now has managed to develop a 5000 sq. yards 4-storey building with a playground, lawn tennis court, skating ring, swimming pool, adventure swings, amphitheatre, pedestrian path and most importantly a variety of flora spread across it. The school has also had some remarkable innings in the past few years including the International School Award 2020-23 and Green Leaf Award by Centre for Science & Environment.

Responsibility and Duties

Teachers
Responsibility Duties
Report to the school before 8 am. Finish the syllabus before time and give enough time for revision in class.
Manage the students and their doubts well. Listen to the parents' requests carefully and work on them accordingly.
Teach with utmost sincerity. Guide the parents carefully, time to time, so that they don't ignore their child's education.
Do not hit a a student ever. Follow the code of conduct.
Be responsible with your outfit. Sarees to be worn on special occasions.
Reach every class on time so that the students' syllabus do not suffer. To leave the job, you are required to submit the required application three months prior leaving.
Do not carry your mobile phone to the class as you can't talk to anyone in between the class. Follow the agreement between you and the school authorities always or else action would be taken.
